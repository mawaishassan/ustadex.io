Frequently Asked Questions

Why does everything start with Book Demo?

Ustadex is designed around decision workflows. A short conversation ensures correct setup and faster value.

How do AI tokens work?

Tokens are consumed as AI features run. They do not reset and can be purchased anytime.

What happens when tokens are exhausted?

AI features pause, but dashboards and data remain accessible.

What's included in the Starter plan?

The Starter plan includes 2 data sources, 5 dashboards per source, 20,000 rows per data source, and 100K AI tokens per month. The first month is free, and no credit card is required to get started.

How does the annual discount work?

When you choose annual billing, you receive a 30% discount on the monthly rate, making it even more cost-effective for long-term use.
